Description

The brief was to invent or redesign the identity for a facility on Aogashima, a small remote island in the Philippine Sea. My colleague Nidhi Billoray and I focused on Fureai Sauna, a steam sauna fueled by the volcanic steam originating from the island's volcanic activity. We designed the logo for this facility, pictograms for its wayfinding system and patterns to be applied in the interior, on cars, towels, clothes and other merchandizing articles. Further, a steam kettle outside the sauna was redesigned with a new color system.
