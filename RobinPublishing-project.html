Description

I designed the corporate identity for a publishing house which devotes its publications to various aspects of urban nature like wildlife in large city parks, urban gardening, reestablishment of natural spaces etc. ROBIN Publishing’s mission is to spread appreciation for nature as a crucial component in growing cities that guarantees a healthy lifestyle. The logo of ROBIN Publishing consists of a red bird in front of three dark blue blocks which represent a city silhouette. Both form a drop-shaped unit which stands for the integration of natural elements into urban zones. At the same time, the bird as a symbol for nature embraces the cityscape to indicate how much cities depend on natural areas for a healthy living.
