Description

Type designer Giambattista Bodoni and biologist Charles Darwin were contemporaries. Although they probably never met, both made a huge impact in their respective fields. I liked the idea of the famous typeface intertwining with the life of the great British naturalist and his journey around the globe that resulted in one of the most impactful ideas ever established: the theory of the evolution of life that forever changed our perspective on the living world.
